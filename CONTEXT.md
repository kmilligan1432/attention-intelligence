# Barbra Bannon Milligan — Site Context for AI Editors

## Who This Site Is For
This is the personal brand website for **Barbra Bannon Milligan** — speaker, consultant, and founder. The site lives at **barbramilligan.com** and is built as a single HTML file (`index.html`) with supporting media in the `/images/` folder.

The site funnels leads into two business lines:
- **Speaking engagements** — keynotes and workshops on Attention Intelligence, AI, and human connection
- **Consulting** — business systems and AI strategy via Oliza.ai and Spark Evolution

## Brand Voice
- Warm, direct, and human — never corporate
- Confident without being boastful
- Speaks to entrepreneurs, business owners, and working parents
- Core message: AI should make you more human, not less
- Tagline territory: "Attention Intelligence for Life's Hardest Moments"

## What to Be Careful With
- Do NOT change font families (Cormorant Garamond + Jost — brand fonts)
- Do NOT change the color palette (forest green + cream + copper)
- Do NOT rename or move image files without updating src references in the HTML
- Do NOT remove the Calendly embed once it is added
